About

Pentalist is developing a revolutionary new way of evaluating candidates for job opportunities. We are working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, cognitive science and management to match students with employment opportunities.

We dont care about your GPA, or your job experience, or your embellished CV. We evaluate candidates based on metrics that matter. After our assesment we will reach out to employers and help them evaluate you using the data you provided. You can focus on other things that matter while we do all the legwork for you.

If you are interested and eligible for our beta, you can sign up.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

pentalist is absolutely free for job applicants.

How can pentalist help you?

First and foremost, your pentalist profile will represent you more objectively. Second, our assesment allows employers to skip the first application rounds. This can save you plenty of time and allow you to apply to a lot more places.

Who will evaluate you?

After your identity is anonymized, our software will help experts in fields ranging from hiring and management to cognitive science and philosophy analyze your application.

Why are employers excited about this?

Due to sheer application volume, employers use simplistic filters like GPA cutoffs. As a result, they may reject promising applicants. With the help of our technology, employers can use more sophisticated filtering techniques that will not have that problem.

What if you don't know what job you want?

Our technology allows us to find your true potential. As a result, we can match you with incredible opportunities that you never even knew existed.

The process

Step 1 Complete the profile

The profile contains basic questions that help us undestand who you are and what you are looking for.

Step 2 Pentalist assesment

Our assesment is an in-house developed set of questionaires that evaluate your cognitive skills, problem solving abilites and temperament, among other traits. This is based on the most ground breaking research in cognitive science.

Step 3

Your online activity

If provided, we anlayze your social media content in order to make a profile based on your personality and interests. This will help us determine whether you are a right fit for a future employer's culture, or find opoortunities in a completely new field for you.

Step 4 Technical interview

Whether you are applying for a finance, consulting or tech job, we want to assess how well you perform on a domain specific test. These tests will be conducted over the phone with experts in the domain.

Step 5 Personal interview

Finally, the most important step. We want to have a conversation with you so we can learn cool things about you that would have otherwise escaped us.
