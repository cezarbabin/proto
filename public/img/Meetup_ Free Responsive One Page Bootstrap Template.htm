About

The job application process sucks for both employers and future employees. Tens of interviews, hundreds of applications and months of search. At Pentalist we want to solve this problems, and evaluate all candidates in an objective way. You only have to complete one application, and we'll do the rest.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

Pentalist is absolutely free for job applicants.

How is different than the status quo?

Interviewers get a better sense of who you are. You don't get evaluated based on your GPA, extracurriculars or past domain experience but rather on how you think and your personality traits.

Who will evaluate me?

After your identity is anonymized, top-class experts in hiring, management, business and even anthropology and philosophy will evaluate your application.

Our technology allows us to find your true potential. As a result, we can match you with incredible opportunities that you never even knew existed.
